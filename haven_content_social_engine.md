
# Haven Study — Social Media & Content Engine

Version: 1.0
Purpose: Align Haven blog posts with social media content to consistently grow awareness, trust, and traffic.

Primary site: https://havenstudy.app

This document complements the **haven_seo_growth_plan.md** file and focuses on:
• Content distribution
• Social media consistency
• Blog → social repurposing
• Weekly publishing cadence

This should be used as a **living playbook**.

---

# 1. Core Strategy

Every blog post should generate multiple social posts.

Content flow:

Blog post → Instagram → Facebook → Twitter/X

This multiplies the reach of each article.

Example:

1 blog post =  
• 1 Instagram post  
• 1 Facebook post  
• 1 Twitter/X post  
• 1 additional tip post later

---

# 2. Weekly Content Rhythm

Recommended weekly cadence:

Monday

# 5. Instagram Post Template

Structure:

Hook

Helpful tip

Conversation starter

Example:

Preparing for the Life in the UK test?

One of the biggest mistakes people make is leaving the test until the last minute.

Taking it earlier removes a lot of stress from the ILR process.

If you're studying now, what part feels hardest — remembering the facts or staying consistent?

# 6. Facebook Post Template
