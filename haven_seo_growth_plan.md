Version: 1.0  
Purpose: Improve SEO visibility, social sharing, and organic discovery for Haven Study while keeping the main application unchanged.

Primary website: https://havenstudy.app

This document defines:

• Technical SEO improvements  
• Metadata updates  
• Social sharing optimisation  
• Blog architecture

This document is intended to be maintained and updated over time and used as a living guide for implementing the Haven SEO and content strategy.

---

# 1. Core Principles

Haven Study is a study platform for the **Life in the UK test**.

SEO strategy focuses on:

• answering real questions people search for  
• providing helpful educational content  
• building topical authority  
• linking blog content into the Haven app

Important constraints:

• Do NOT change existing application routes  
• Do NOT disrupt the core study experience  
• Blog content should sit outside the main app functionality

---

# 2. Canonical Domain Strategy

Choose **one primary domain**.

Recommended:

https://havenstudy.app

All other domains should redirect to this.

Examples:

haven.study → havenstudy.app  
www.havenstudy.app → havenstudy.app

Add canonical tag to all pages:

<link rel="canonical" href="https://havenstudy.app/">

Purpose:

• prevents SEO duplication  
• consolidates domain authority

# 4. Open Graph (Social Sharing)

Open Graph tags control previews on:

• Facebook  
• LinkedIn  
• WhatsApp  
• Slack

Add to <head>:

<meta property="og:title" content="Life in the UK Test Practice & Study Guide | Haven Study">
<meta property="og:description" content="Prepare for the Life in the UK test with clear lessons, realistic practice questions, and simple study tools. Study calmly and pass with confidence.">
<meta property="og:image" content="https://havenstudy.app/social-preview.png">
<meta property="og:url" content="https://havenstudy.app">
<meta property="og:type" content="website">

Recommended preview image size:

1200 × 630 px

# 9. Blog Article Template

Each article should follow this structure:

Intro paragraph  
Direct answer  
Detailed explanation  
Common mistakes  
Study tips

CTA to Haven Study

Tone:

• calm  
• reassuring  
• practical

Avoid:

• aggressive marketing  
• legal immigration advice

# 12. Weekly Publishing Workflow

Suggested weekly process:

1. Identify a real question people ask
2. Write one blog post
3. Share on Instagram, Facebook, and X
4. Answer one community question online
5. Link readers back to Haven

Approximate weekly time investment:

60 minutes
